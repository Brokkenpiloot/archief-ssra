# Site brief (draft)

## Goal

Create a clear, accessible website for **Archief SSRA**. The final hosting and stack are not known yet.

## Audience

- Members and alumni of SSRA

## Tone

- Professional
- Clear and straightforward
- Trustworthy and archival

## Homepage (draft)

- Simple landing page with welcome message for members and alumni
- Explains the archive purpose in one short paragraph
- Classic login form with email and password fields
- "Forgot my password" and "Request access" buttons (not yet functional)

## Constraints (current)

- No DNS or hosting access yet
- Stack not decided
- Work is focused on structure and content planning

## Success criteria

- Visitors can quickly find the archive, context, and contact info
- Site works well on mobile and desktop
- Archival content is not publicly available, but securely locked behind a log-in flow
- Members and alumni can log-in and get access to the archival content
- Members and alumni get invited to the archive through a list of emailadresses which we wil supply
- The archive is a collection of pdf files that will be very simply ordered based on year and type of file
